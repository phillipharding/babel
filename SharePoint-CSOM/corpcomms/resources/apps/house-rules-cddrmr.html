    Our day-to-day work is guided by external regulation. It is the accountability of all colleagues to know the regulations that apply to their particular area of work, and abide by them. If you are in any doubt or would just like to be refreshed of the relevant and current regulations in your area, please speak to your local or global legal team member.

    As a company, we are commited to observing the laws and regulations which govern our operations in every country where we do business.
